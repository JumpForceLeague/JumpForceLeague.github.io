League Rules & Guidelines

1. LEAGUE STRUCTURE

  • League Schedule –
    With only 8 teams in this league, that would make for 7 weeks of league play.After that 7 weeks, we will be holding a two week “Grudge Match” session. After that there will be playoffs.
  • Grudge Matches–
    Grudge matches are held after the main season for a two-week period.
  • Divisions –
    There will be two divisions in this league. Heart and Soul division. This is basically an upper and lower tier system.
  • Major/Minor League Playoffs –
    Playoffs will basically start from semi-finals of a tournament bracket. There will be two separate playoffs going on at the same time. One is for the top 2 teams in each division and the other is for the bottom 2 teams in each division.

2. TEAM GUIDELINES

  • Teams –
    There are 8 teams to choose from with each team having a base roster of 4 characters. They will have an addition of a free agent each season.
  • Team Representatives –
    Team representative will be voted on internally by each team. They will act as a lead with the following responsibilities: publishing final builds, breaking tie breakers, and finalizing trade deals. Supporters can call for a revote at ANY time with staff approval. People who abuse these powers will be suspended from being their team’s representative.
  • Team Divinity –
    If a team goes a round without taking any damage or wins 3 rounds in a row without going past the 50% health threshold, that team will be immortalized as “Divine”. This is a system of Elite status.

3. MATCH SETUP

  • Matches –
    The Match setup will be a 3v3 best of 5 matches. The line up will be 3 characters chosen by the team for each league with builds.
  • Builds and Lineups –
    Builds will be a 5-slot system. Lineups will be set up in a way that there are two benches each week, your Exhausted Member and your Revitalized Member. Revitalized Members will be force into the lineups the next week and the Exhausted Member will be force into the Revitalized Member slot. There are not rules stating each character needs to be benched, but when someone is benched that means they will be benched for two weeks in a row.
  • Skills –
    Unlike other leagues, each character has access to their own individual set of skills. They will be allocated as slots similar to SSBL.

4. CURRENCY

  • Credits –
    Credits are the form of currency for this league. At the start of season 1, each team will receive 10 credits. For every match a team wins, you will receive 3 credits. For every match a team loses, you will receive 5 credits.
  • Playoff Rewards -
    The minor league champion will receive 3 credits and major league champion receiving 5 credits. Anyone in Soul division will receive 5 credits at the end of the season with Heart only getting 2 credits.
  • Betting -
    Teams may place bets on the victors of each match. They can bet to a max of 5 and receive a 2x multiplier rounded up to the bet if they win. Losing results in the bet being claimed by the house. Bets can only be placed against the house and you cannot bet against your own team.

5. OFFSEASON

  • Drop/Add –
    When dropping a character, a team must “Sell” the character to the staff. They will offer you a price and you can accept, deny, or counter it. Any character you add to you team must be bought via credits. You may direct trade to other teams or buy the rights to one of their characters. Prices vary based on team performance, character skill, and other factors.
  • Free Agents –
    Free agents are picked up the same way as added characters. If a team cannot purchase a free agent, the staff will lone them the character. However, the staff chooses which character and the team will receive pay a 10-credit fee at the end of the season.
  • Testing -
    When the season is over, testing will follow soon after. Tests will vary in types, however, keep in mind the same character cannot have different builds at different times.
